How can we measure sub-femtonewton optical forces?

An optically tweezed free-floating microsphere is able to sense extremely small forces in fluid, with sensitivity limited only by fundamental thermal fluctuations.

How common are the Magellanic Clouds?

The sloan digital sky survey is the largest database of galactic objects ever created, making the actual universe ripe for statistical study. However, line-of-sight distance is measured only with redshift, which is unavailable for most objects in the database.
